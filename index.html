About me

My Dev's Path

01. Origins

I'm not the typical developer. A long time ago, my journey began — over 20 years ago — as a young padawan discovering the early powers of visuals, motion, and detail. First came pixel-perfect layouts, smooth interactions, and interfaces that felt alive. Then came the other side — solving challenges, building integrations, and connecting systems. Some call it the dark side of development. I call it balance.

02. Now

Today, I walk the path of product design — blending strategy, UX thinking and fullstack execution. It's not just about how to build — but what, why and for whom.

03. Forward

Learning never stops. I stay curious, adaptable and ready for what's next. I believe in building things that last — products with purpose and tools I'd want to use myself.
